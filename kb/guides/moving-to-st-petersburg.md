## Planned structure (question-phrased H2s)

1. **Where is what? The 20-minute map of the city.** Downtown/waterfront, the historic core,
   the west side, the beaches (separate municipalities), the Skyway/south. Descriptive
   geography only — place attributes, never resident profiling (chassis rule).
2. **What does the weather actually do?** Real seasons (summer storm pattern, hurricane season
   June–Nov), what Helene/Milton did and changed, evacuation zones vs. flood zones (they
   differ — a common newcomer confusion).
3. **What does it cost to live here?** Property-tax mechanics (homestead exemption, Save Our
   Homes cap, the non-homestead reset at purchase — the "same house, double the taxes"
   surprise), insurance as the real line item, no state income tax.
4. **How do people get around?** Car reality, the SunRunner BRT, the airport pair (PIE/TPA),
   the Howard Frankland commute to Tampa.
5. **How do I actually land?** Rent-first vs. buy-now trade, school-enrollment mechanics
   (attendance zones cited to the district's official locator ONLY — no school ratings and
   no school-quality adjectives, ever: steering-adjacent), and the 90-day checklist.

## Sourcing plan (verify at drafting — nothing below ships from memory)

- Geography/districts: city official pages (stpete.org), St. Pete EDC.
- Weather/storms: NWS Tampa Bay climate normals; NHC reports for Helene/Milton; city updates
  (already in kb/facts/flood.yaml); Pinellas "Know Your Zone" for evacuation zones.
- Taxes: Pinellas County Property Appraiser (homestead, Save Our Homes, portability
  calculators — pcpao.gov); FL DOR. Every figure with its statute/page cite + date.
- Transit: PSTA (SunRunner pages), FDOT for the Howard Frankland status.
- Schools: Pinellas County Schools official attendance-zone locator link only.
- Cross-links: flood guide (flagship), buying guide (#4), neighborhoods guide (#2).

**Fair-housing note (bound):** relocation guides are the classic steering trap. This one
describes places, costs, and mechanics. No demographic characterizations of areas, no school
quality claims — link the official locator and stop.
